# Acme Corp — Employee Handbook (Excerpt)

## Working hours and time off

Standard working hours at Acme Corp are 9:00 to 18:00, Monday to Friday.
Employees may take up to 25 paid vacation days per calendar year, plus
public holidays observed in their country of residence. Vacation requests
must be submitted at least two weeks in advance through the HR portal and
require approval from the employee's direct manager.

Unused vacation days can be carried over to the following year, up to a
maximum of five days. Any excess is forfeited on March 31st.

## Remote work policy

Acme Corp supports a hybrid working model. Employees are expected to be
present at the office at least two days per week, typically Tuesday and
Thursday. Fully remote arrangements may be approved by the People team on
a case-by-case basis, provided the role does not require physical
presence.

When working remotely, employees must use the company VPN for any access
to internal systems. Personal devices are not permitted for handling
customer data.

## Expense reimbursement

Business expenses incurred while travelling for work are reimbursed in
full. Receipts must be submitted within 30 days of the trip via the
finance portal. Meal allowances are capped at 60 EUR per day in Europe
and 90 USD per day in North America.

Software subscriptions and learning resources up to 500 EUR per year are
also covered, subject to manager approval.
